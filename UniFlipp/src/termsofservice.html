Terms of Service

1. User Registration

The website provides functionality for user registration. Non-member users can sign up and become members by providing a username, email address, full name, date of birth, city, and password.

2. Browse and Search

Users, both members and non-members, can browse a list of school items sorted by school, course, type, newest, alphabetical order, and price. They can also search for specific school items by close name match.

3. Listing School Items

Members can list their own school items on the site, including detailed information such as price, item type, description, city of location, and quality condition (new, like new, written in, or damaged).

4. Donations

Users are given the option to donate to support the website.

5. Information Accessibility

Information about the website's purpose, mission, and contact details are accessible.

6. Profile Management

Members can manage their profiles by updating personal information such as name, birthday, and city.

7. Detailed Item Information

Detailed information about each listed school item, including price, item type description, city of location, quality condition, and any additional metadata, is displayed when an item is selected.

8. Purchasing and Negotiation

Purchasing items is done privately between the owner and the member, with negotiation of trades facilitated with respect and kindness. Chat logs are recorded.

9. Notification

Owners are notified via email of purchase inquiries. A page listing all currently pending and completed chats is accessible.

10. Local Businesses

Local businesses are provided with a different class of account allowing them to upload CSV files containing lists of school items. Their accounts are linked to a website serving as the automatic redirect for school items listed by the business.

By using this website, you agree to abide by these Terms of Service. Failure to comply may result in account suspension or termination.
